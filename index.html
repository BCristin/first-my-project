Event Photography

Event photography is a type of photography that involves photographing an event. The event can be anything from a wedding, to a concert, to a ball game.

Portrait Photography

Portrait photography is a very well-known and popular style of photography. This style of photography is all about trying to capture the personality, identity, soul, and emotions of a person by utilizing the background atmosphere, poses, and lighting.

Wedding Photography

Wedding photographers capture memories for brides and grooms, creating a photographic narrative of wedding ceremonies. They take action shots of pre-ceremony preparations, the marriage ceremony, and the reception. They also take posed shots of the bride, groom, wedding party, and family members..
